Proof of IEEE Membership and Student Status

Participants paying the IEEE or IEEE SPS membership rates for workshop registration must provide their membership number for verification purposes. Inability to provide a membership number or an expired membership status will result in the participant being charged the full, non-member registration rate. Non-IEEE student registration will only be accepted with a proof of student status, which can be a copy of your student identification card or a letter from your institution. Please send such proof of student status via email to the publicity chair.

Registration Policy for Authors

At least one author of a paper must register by October 23, 2017 at a regular (non-student and non-life member) rate, and one of the authors must present the paper in person.
